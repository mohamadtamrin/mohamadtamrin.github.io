About Me

Mohamad Tamrin is a founder of Suluh Tenggara Institute, a writer and researcher who focuses on the study of digital technology, artificial intelligence, and natural language processing (NLP). Tamrin graduated from the Department of Statistics at the Islamic University of Indonesia in Yogyakarta. Then, in 2020, he obtained a M.Sc degree after completing his study at the Department of Computer Science and Econometrics - Big Data Analytics from Warsaw University of Life Sciences, Poland. In addition, he has also studied (non-degree) at the School of Polish Language and Culture, University of Silesia, in Katowice, Poland. From 2021 until now, he has been active as a lecturer at one of the universities in Mataram city. In 2022, Tamrin obtained a DANA INDONESIANA (Kemendikbud) grant with the theme of reading the digitization practice of Lontar manuscripts. In this research, Tamrin highlights the involvement of digital technology in the preservation of Lontar manuscripts, including the discourse within them.
